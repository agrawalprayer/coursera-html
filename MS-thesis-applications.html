Introduction

In the final year of your BS-MS degree, you have to submit an original Master's thesis. This page will guide on the application project for Master's thesis in India and abroad.

Field of Interest

The first step to applying for MS Thesis is identifying your field(s) of interest. You don't have to be very specific; knowing a broad range of interests will also be good enough, to begin with.

Finding Labs and Potential Supervisors

The next step is finding labs that work in your area of interest. Go to the institutional websites, and navigate to the department's page, read through the research work of different groups and note down labs of your interest. Good organizational skills come handy here. Create an excel sheet with names of labs, links to their webpages, names of supervisor(s), their email addresses, and status of your application (emailed, discussed, offered etc).
